# Moon Cat Master Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: complete layered-animation character master for a magical summoning website

Primary request: Create one original small floating moonlight spirit cat. It should feel rounded and cute yet quiet and mysterious, suitable for hovering just outside a magical girl's shoulder without covering her face.

Subject: Three-quarter frontal view of a complete compact cat with all four legs, both ears, and the entire tail visible. Use deep violet and silver-white as the main colors, pale blue eyes, an original lunar-phase forehead marking, a tail whose tip naturally curls into a crescent silhouette, and restrained stardust gradients at the ear tips and paws. Keep the silhouette clean and readable. The cat has no wings and no collar.

Style/medium: Polished semi-painted animation illustration, softly dimensional but easy to separate into body, head, eyes, ears, tail, and glow layers.

Composition/framing: One complete subject only, centered, generous padding on every side, no cropped limbs or tail. Calm hovering pose with a compact body and a clear neck, ear roots, tail root, back, and limb contours that can support gentle 2D rigging.

Lighting/mood: Unified soft moonlight from the upper left; serene, enchanted, companionable.

Color palette: Deep violet, silver-white, pale blue, restrained moon-gold highlights. Do not use chroma green anywhere in the subject.

Scene/backdrop: Perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background for background removal. The background must be one uniform color with no shadows, gradients, texture, reflections, floor plane, or lighting variation.

Constraints: Generate exactly one complete mother image, not a parts sheet and not separate components. Crisp subject edges with generous padding. Original design only. No cast shadow, contact shadow, reflection, text, watermark, logo, religious symbol, cross-shaped collar, or traits of an existing named character.

Avoid: wings, accessories that obscure the neck or ear roots, exaggerated action pose, cropped tail, extra animals, scenery, separate anatomy callouts, green spill, blurry outline.
