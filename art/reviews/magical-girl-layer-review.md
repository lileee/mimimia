## Result

Approved for animation and runtime export.

The pack was produced only by separating and repairing the approved original master. No new image generation was used during the split. Hidden overlap paint was added where moving hair, cape, skirt, and staff pieces could otherwise reveal gaps. The closed-eye layer and staff glow are derived additions for animation.

Both review boards were inspected at neutral and both motion extremes, at 1× and 2× scale, against black, white, and deep-purple backgrounds. Hair-to-face, hat-to-hair, cape-to-arm, skirt-to-leg, hand-to-staff, eye swap, and glow overlap all remain continuous. No visible holes, doubled outlines, green or white edge fringe, ordering errors, or unacceptable deformation were found within the declared motion ranges.
